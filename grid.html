About Me

Hi, I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate at The University of Texas at Austin. I major in mathematics, and my research interest include analysis, partial differential equations, especially fluid equations, including incompressible Euler equations, Navier-Stokes equations and surface quasi-geostrophic equations. I received my bachelor’s degree at Xi’an Jiaotong University, where I was also a student of the Special Class for the Gifted Young before my undergraduate study.

Apart from mathematics, I also love programming and music.

It is my honor to be coadvised by Prof. Caffarelli and Prof. Vasseur.
